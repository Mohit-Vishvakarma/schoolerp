School Policies & Terms

Admission Policy

  • Admission is open to all eligible students regardless of caste, creed, or religion
  • Age criteria must be met as per CBSE guidelines
  • Admission forms must be submitted with all required documents
  • Entrance test may be conducted for certain grades
  • Admission is subject to availability of seats

Fee Policy

  • Fees must be paid by the 10th of each month
  • Late fee charges apply after due date
  • Fee structure is reviewed annually
  • Scholarships available for meritorious students
  • Fee concessions available for economically weaker sections

Attendance Policy

  • Minimum 75% attendance required for examination eligibility
  • Medical certificates required for absence due to illness
  • Parents notified for irregular attendance
  • Make-up classes available for genuine cases

Code of Conduct

  • Students must maintain discipline and decorum
  • Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students
  • No bullying or ragging tolerated
  • Proper uniform and appearance mandatory
  • Mobile phones not allowed in classrooms

Terms of Use (Website)

  • All content is protected by copyright
  • Personal information collected is kept confidential
  • Website usage implies acceptance of these terms
  • School reserves right to modify policies
