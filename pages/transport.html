Transport Guidelines

  • Students must reach the designated pick-up point 5 minutes before scheduled time
  • Parents must inform the transport coordinator about any changes in routine
  • Students are not allowed to change routes without prior permission
  • Transport fees must be paid by the 5th of each month
  • In case of medical emergency, contact the transport attendant immediately
  • Parents can track their child's bus location via the school app
  • Students must wear seat belts at all times
  • No eating or drinking inside the bus
  • Respect the bus attendant and follow their instructions
  • Report any concerns regarding transport service to the school office
