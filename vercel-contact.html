Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

Visit our admission page and fill the online form, or contact the admission office directly. We conduct entrance tests for classes VI, IX, and XI.

What are the fee payment options?

We accept online payments via bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and digital wallets through our ERP system.

Is there transportation facility?

Yes, we have 20+ buses covering all major areas of the city with trained drivers and GPS tracking.

How can students access the ERP portal?

Students can login using their roll number and password to access marks, attendance, homework, and other academic information.
